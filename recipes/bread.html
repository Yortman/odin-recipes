Cheddar Bread

Cheddar Bread is cheesy, soft, and satisfies all of those savory bread cravings. It`s super easy and ready in just 90 minutes!

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 tablespoons salted butter
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons Frank's Hot Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Egg Wash

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Melt the butter over low heat in a small saucepan. Add in buttermilk and water and heat until it is just warm to the touch.
  2. Transfer the liquids into the bowl of a stand mixer. Add in the yeast. Whisk in the 3 eggs and hot sauce.
  3. Add in the sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt on a low setting to combine.
  4. Add in the 4 cups of flour and knead on a medium setting until well combined. Stir in the parmesan cheese and 3/4 cup of the cheddar cheese.
  5. Divide the dough in two and place each half into a greased bread pan.
  6. Let rise 45 minutes. After the dough rises, whisk together 1 egg + 1 tablespoon water in a small bowl. Brush over the tops of each loaf.
  7. Bake at 375 degrees for 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with the remaining 1/4 cup cheddar cheese. Bake an additional 10 minutes.
